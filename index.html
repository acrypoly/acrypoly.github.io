Speciality

We take privilage in introducing ourselves as one of leading Acrylics/ Plastics Sheets Online Information, Buying and Selling all varities of sheets.

  • Acrylic Sheet

    Cast acrylic sheet is a material with unique physical properties. It weighs half as much as the finest optical glass, yet is equal to it in clarity and is up to 17 times more impact resistant. Cast acrylic sheet is made in over 250 colors, in thicknesses from .030" to 4.25' and can transmit ultraviolet light or filter it out, as required.

  • Polycarbonate Multiwall

    Multiwall panels blend improved qualities of both polycarbonate and multiwall design to offer great versatility for usage. They are most commonly used for roofing, cladding, glazing and siding as well as many other architectural, constructional and advertising applications. Polycarbonate multiwall sheet are most often used for greenhouse.

  • Polysterne Sheets

    Polystyrene (PS) is used for producing disposable plastic cutlery and dinnerware, CD "jewel" cases, smoke detector housings, license plate frames, plastic model assembly kits, and many other objects where a rigid, economical plastic is desired.Polystyrene is used in construction, laboratory equipment and food packaging.

  • PVC/ Foam Board

    PVC foam board, also known as Chevron board and Andy board, its chemical composition is polyvinyl chloride. Widely used in buses, train cars ceiling, box core, interior panels, building wall panels, decorative panels, office, residential, public building, commercial decorative frame, clean room with a plate , ceiling panels.

  • Embossed Sheets

    PC LITE Embossed Sheet has several features with high impact resistance, light weight, can be easy cold bend processed and hot molding. Hence, it can be widely used in construction and decorations.The polycarbonate sheets come in a wide range of shapes and designs such as the embossed etc.

  • PP Flute Board (Corrugated Sheets)

    Offering Flute Board, which are used in varied industry segments, such as protective packaging, signage and construction, due to its better hinge properties. The light weight PP Flute Boards is mainly used in Protective packaging of expensive and sensitive spares.
